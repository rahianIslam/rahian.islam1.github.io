I'm a Mechanical Engineer (EIT) and graduate of the Schulich School of Engineering at the University of Calgary — B.Sc. Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Digital Engineering, graduated December 2024. Currently working at Tarco Consulting Inc. supporting multi-disciplinary oil & gas projects for clients including TC Energy and Enbridge.

My engineering career spans turbomachinery fleet management, piping and equipment design, ASME/CSA/API code compliance, FMEA analysis, and technical documentation — built through two internship terms and a full-time EIT role across compressor stations, pipelines, and processing facilities.

Before university, I co-founded a tutoring centre serving over 250 students and a nonprofit in Dhaka, Bangladesh. These early ventures shaped a leadership style grounded in accountability, strategic thinking, and a relentless bias for action.
